Program Overview

Our MBA program is designed for ambitious professionals who want to lead in the digital age. We combine traditional business fundamentals with cutting-edge technology insights to prepare leaders who can navigate and drive digital transformation across industries.

With a unique blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, our curriculum emphasizes innovation, strategic thinking, and ethical leadership in the global marketplace.

Admission Requirements

üìã Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree from recognized university
  • Minimum 2.5 CGPA or 60% aggregate
  • 2+ years work experience (preferred but not mandatory)
  • Valid GMAT/CAT/MAT score (or NIT entrance test)
  • English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS for international students)

üìÑ Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts
  • Statement of Purpose (1000 words)
  • 2 Letters of Recommendation
  • Resume/CV
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Copy of citizenship/passport

Step-by-Step Process:

  1. Submit online application with required documents
  2. Appear for entrance examination (if required)
  3. Attend personal interview (shortlisted candidates)
  4. Await admission decision (within 2 weeks)
  5. Confirm admission with fee payment

Important Dates:

  • Spring Intake: Applications due December 15
  • Fall Intake: Applications due July 15
  • Entrance Test: January & August
  • Interviews: January & August

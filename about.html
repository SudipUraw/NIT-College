Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Nepal's leading technology institute.

ğŸ›ï¸ Our Foundation

Established in 2013 with a vision to revolutionize technology education in Nepal, NIT started with just 50 students and a handful of passionate faculty members. Our founders recognized the growing need for technology professionals in Nepal's developing economy.

What began as a small institute has grown into a comprehensive university offering cutting-edge programs in business administration and information technology, serving over 2,500 students from across Nepal and beyond.

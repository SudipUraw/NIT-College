Admission Process

Simple, transparent, and designed to find the best candidates

1

Online Application

Submit your application form online with required documents. Our user-friendly portal makes it easy to apply.

  • Fill application form
  • Upload documents
  • Pay application fee
  • Get confirmation email
2

Entrance Examination

Take our comprehensive entrance exam testing your academic foundation and analytical skills.

  • Mathematics & Logic
  • English Proficiency
  • General Knowledge
  • Critical Thinking
3

Personal Interview

One-on-one interview with our faculty to assess your motivation, goals, and fit for the program.

  • Academic discussion
  • Career aspirations
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Communication abilities
4

Final Selection

Results announced based on overall performance. Successful candidates proceed with enrollment.

  • Merit-based selection
  • Results notification
  • Enrollment process
  • Welcome orientation

Programs & Requirements

Choose your path and review admission requirements for each program

MBA Admission Requirements

Eligibility

  • Bachelor's degree in any field with minimum 50% marks
  • Minimum 2 years of work experience preferred
  • Age limit: 35 years maximum
  • English proficiency (IELTS 6.5+ for international students)

Selection Criteria

  • Entrance Exam: 40% weightage
  • Personal Interview: 30% weightage
  • Academic Record: 20% weightage
  • Work Experience: 10% weightage

MBA Program Details

Duration 2 Years (4 Semesters)
Mode Full-time / Part-time
Intake Spring (February) & Fall (August)
Seats 120 per intake
Application Fee NPR 2,000
Tuition Fee NPR 400,000 per year

Required Documents

  • Bachelor's degree transcripts
  • Work experience certificates
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Citizenship certificate
  • Character certificate
  • Medical certificate

B.Sc IT Admission Requirements

Eligibility

  • +2 or equivalent with minimum 60% marks
  • Science background preferred (not mandatory)
  • Age limit: 17-25 years
  • Basic computer literacy recommended

Selection Criteria

  • Entrance Exam: 50% weightage
  • +2 Academic Record: 30% weightage
  • Personal Interview: 20% weightage

B.Sc IT Program Details

Duration 4 Years (8 Semesters)
Mode Full-time
Intake Fall (August) - Main Intake
Seats 200 per year
Application Fee NPR 1,500
Tuition Fee NPR 300,000 per year

Required Documents

  • +2 transcripts and certificate
  • SEE certificate
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Citizenship certificate
  • Character certificate from +2 institution
  • Medical fitness certificate

Program Comparison

Aspect MBA Program B.Sc IT Program
Target Students Working professionals, entrepreneurs High school graduates, career starters
Focus Areas Business management, leadership, strategy Programming, AI, cybersecurity, data science

About this Field

The Linguistics field at NIT is dedicated to understanding language through a computational lens. We focus on Natural Language Processing (NLP), teaching computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language. Students work on exciting projects like sentiment analysis, machine translation, and chatbot development, preparing for careers in a core area of modern AI.

Key Focus Areas

  • Computational Linguistics
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Corpus Linguistics & Text Analysis
  • Phonetics & Speech Recognition
  • Syntax, Semantics & Parsing Algorithms

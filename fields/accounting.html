About this Field

Our Accounting field goes beyond traditional bookkeeping. We emphasize the use of technology in financial analysis, auditing, and management. Students learn to leverage data analytics and financial software to make strategic business decisions, preparing them for roles in the rapidly evolving world of FinTech and corporate finance.

Key Focus Areas

  • Financial Technology (FinTech)
  • Data Analytics for Accountants
  • Forensic Accounting & Auditing
  • International Financial Reporting
  • Strategic Managerial Accounting

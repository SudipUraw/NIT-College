About this Field

At NIT, the Mathematics field is centered on its application in technology. We explore the core mathematical principles that power data science, cryptography, and artificial intelligence. Our approach combines rigorous theoretical study with practical, computational problem-solving, equipping students with the analytical skills essential for innovation.

Key Focus Areas

  • Discrete Mathematics & Algorithms
  • Probability & Statistics for Data Science
  • Cryptography & Number Theory
  • Linear Algebra for Machine Learning
  • Mathematical Modeling & Simulation
